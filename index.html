'Solar_Powered Evaporator's Presentation Context

Project Objectives

  • Utilize renewable energy
  • Achieve high efficiency and scalability
  • Prioritize portability and cost-effectiveness

Current Semester Goals

  1. Material procurement and prototype construction
  2. Completion of basic design framework
  3. Performance testing and evaluation
  4. Identification of priority requirements and expected impacts

Annual Objectives

  • Design and optimize an efficient solar evaporator system
  • Achieve near 100% evaporation efficiency
  • Maintain TDS below 500 mg/L
  • Maintain pH between 6 and 8

Deliverables

  • Complete solar evaporator prototype
  • Detailed concept design statement
  • Feasibility study and performance evaluation
  • Design documents (including CAD drawings, assembly instructions, and parts list)
  • Detailed final design report

Validation Plan

  • Structural strength testing
  • Water quality testing
  • Evaporation rate measurement and adaptability to light variations
  • Energy efficiency assessment

Current Progress

  1. Preliminary technical analysis
  2. Design and refinement of design concepts
  3. Detailed technical analysis of key design features (in progress)
  4. Selection of final design concept
